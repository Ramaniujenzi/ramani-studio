🎨 MoodMorph – Architectural Moodboard Studio

MoodMorph is a responsive, browser-based moodboard app crafted for visual thinkers who sketch with style and structure. Built by FUE, it blends expressive brushes, architectural textures, cinematic palettes, and real-time interaction.

Where linework meets light. Crafted with chaos and clarity.

💡 Features

  • 🖌️ Brush Engine: Scribble, Hatch, Glow, Freehand + live previews
  • 🧱 Draggable Assets: Arrange textures, tags, and palettes freely
  • 🌗 Light/Dark Mode: Toggle visual atmosphere
  • 💾 Export Tools: PNG, SVG, and full moodboard capture
  • ⏪ Undo/Redo: Stroke history control
  • 🔖 Style Tags & Notes: Label and log your inspiration
